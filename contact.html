Contact


Positions We are interested in recruiting highly motivated undergraduates, graduate students, and postdocs who share our scientific curiosity and interests.

Undergraduates: please e-mail the PI with your resume and a paragraph describing your academic trajectory and your specific interest in our lab. Hourly (work-study), research credit, and other arrangements will be considered. Undergraduates should be willing to devote a minimum of 10 hours a week during the semester. Students will also be encouraged to apply for summer research scholarships.

Graduate Students: prospective graduate students should gain admission to one of the graduate programs for which the PI serves as a trainer:
      Comparative Biomedical Sciences (CBMS) Graduate Program
      Cellular and Molecular Pathology (CMP) Graduate Training Program
      Bacteriology MS Degree Program
Students may be eligible for financial support through various mechanisms, including through the Parasitology and Vector Biology Training Program.

Postdoctoral Scholars: please contact the PI directly with your CV and a brief description of project(s) you would like to pursue in our lab. Prospective postdocs are strongly encouraged to seek out potential sources of fellowship funding.
